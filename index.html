Why It Matters

Self-Awareness

Social media can affect your mood and self-image, often without you realizing it. Our tracker helps you notice how you feel after scrolling, so you can understand what lifts you up—and what doesn’t.

Spot Red Flags

If scrolling leaves you feeling drained, annoyed, or like you’re not enough, that’s a red flag. It’s common to compare yourself to others online—even if you know it’s all highlights and filters. Our quiz helps you spot those moments and make changes to make it better.

Pause & Pull Back

It’s easy to get stuck in a scroll spiral. But you can hit pause—literally. We’ve got tips for taking breaks, setting boundaries, and bouncing back when social media gets overwhelming. You’re in charge of your feed, not the other way around.
