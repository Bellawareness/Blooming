Signs of Distress

Identify moments of distress and log your triggers.

Overstimulation

Feeling overwhelmed by sensory input.

Sadness

Feeling a persistent low mood or tearful.

Comparison

Frequently comparing yourself to others negatively.

Irritability

Feeling easily annoyed or frustrated online.

Withdrawal

Wanting to avoid friends or activities you usually enjoy.

Physical Discomfort

Do you notice headaches, tense muscles, or tired eyes while online?
